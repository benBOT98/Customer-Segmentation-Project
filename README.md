# Customer Segment Project

## Project Overview

The focus of this project is to help the business to better understand its customers and makes it easier for them to modify products according to the specific needs, behaviours and concerns of different types of customers.

Customer personality analysis helps a business to modify its product based on its target customers from different types of customer segments. For example, instead of spending money to market a new product to every customer in the company’s database, a company can analyse which customer segment is most likely to buy the product and then market the product only on that particular segment.

## Tools Used 

  - Excel for data cleaning
  - Power BI for data visualisation

## Analysis Report

  - The analysis shows a customer base of 2.24K customers with an average age of 57 years, an average income of 52.2K, and total spending of approximately 1.36M, indicating a relatively mature and financially stable customer segment.
  - Seniors contribute the highest total spending (0.63M), significantly outperforming other age groups, making them the most valuable demographic for targeted marketing campaigns.
  - The Mature Adults segment is the second-highest spender at 0.40M, while Adults contribute the least, suggesting that marketing efforts are currently more effective among older customers.
  - The RFM segmentation reveals that the business has a strong proportion of Loyal Customers, Potential Loyalists, and Need Attention customers, showing good retention potential but also highlighting customers who may require re-engagement strategies.
  - The scatter analysis indicates a positive relationship between income level and total spending, with higher-income customers generally contributing more revenue across RFM segments.
  - Purchase channel analysis shows that Store Purchases slightly dominate overall customer transactions, although Web and Catalog purchases are also well-balanced, suggesting the company successfully operates across multiple sales channels.
